🌿 AgroInsight v2.0

An enterprise-grade AI ecosystem empowering sustainable agriculture through deep learning, predictive analytics, and real-time multilingual data insights.

View Source Code on GitHub

📸 Crop Disease Vision Lab

Utilizes a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) trained on 50,000+ images to instantly detect 26 diseases across 14 crop species with high confidence.

📈 Market Price Forecaster

Implements historical time-series data to forecast commodity price trends and calculate optimal sell windows for maximum profitability.

🧪 Soil Health Analyzer

Evaluates N-P-K and pH metrics to generate precise, AI-driven fertilizer prescriptions tailored to specific target crops.

🤖 AgriBot (Vision Enabled)

A multi-modal AI assistant powered by Google Gemini 2.5 Flash that processes text and images to answer farming queries.

🏛️ Govt Schemes Matcher

Dynamically matches farmers to regional government agricultural schemes based on location, crop type, and farm size.

🌐 Native Localization

Strictly enforces multilingual outputs (Hindi, Telugu, Marathi, Tamil, English) with Text-to-Speech capabilities for maximum accessibility.
